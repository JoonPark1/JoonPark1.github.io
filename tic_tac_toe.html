Sung Park's Tic Tac Toe Demo

Below, I have attached a demo video playing 2 example rounds of tic tac toe game! I hope you enjoy the demo. Also, if you are confused about positioning of cells, make sure to read the description section on home page!

Rules:

1. The player can only place on an empty cell!

2. The player can win if he gets 3 of his marks without getting blocked.

3. If no player wins and all cells are full, the game results in a tie!
